Start the Navigation Quiz

The goal of the navigation quizzess is to improve your familiarity with the MPEP and decrease your look-up time. Please refer to the MPEP

Note that this version of the MPEP only lets you search in each chapter (like the exam). Feel free to search in each chapter. Do not use the completely searchable version of the MPEP for this quiz, because then you will not be adequately prepared for the actual exam.

This is a timed quiz. Your final score will be a composite of the time taken and your accuracy. Your goal should be to spend less than 60 seconds looking up an answer. This is an ambitious goal, initially it will take you much longer.
